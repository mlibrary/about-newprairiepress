## Services Offered
Our conference proceedings publishing services are ideal for Kansas State University affiliated conference organizers seeking a solution to manage proceedings publishing from initial submission through production. Note that only one or more organizers are required to be affiliated with K-State at the time the conference is onboarded with the Press. The conference itself does not need to be affiliated.

New Prairie Press offers the following conference proceedings publishing services:
* A designed site.  
* Software to manage editorial and peer-review workflows, if needed.  
* Ability to embed presentation videos.
* DOI registration.
* Training and technical support.
* Upload of past proceedings.
* Information and advice on copyright and licensing.
