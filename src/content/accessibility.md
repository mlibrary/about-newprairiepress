## Ebook Accessibility
*Last manual evaluation: 2026-04-15*

It is the goal of New Prairie Press that every title we publish follows [WCAG AA](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/) and [EPUB Accessibility](https://www.w3.org/TR/epub-a11y-11/) compliance standards. 

EPUBs and PDFs published prior to 2026 may not conform to WCAG AA and EPUB Accessibility compliance standards. 

All titles we publish are open access, can be freely downloaded, and do not contain DRM restrictions. 

Published content is evaluated using a combination of automated tools and manual processes including, but necessarily limited to, [Ace by DAISY](https://daisy.github.io/ace/) (EPUB), [Ace SMART by DAISY](https://smart.daisy.org/) (EPUB), [PAC Accessibility Checker](https://pac.pdf-accessibility.org/en) (PDF), and Adobe Acrobat (PDF).

## Accessibility of this site
Our baseline accessibility target for this website is to meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards. 

The last accessibility evaluation for [about.newprairiepress.org](https://about.newprairiepress.org) was in April 2026. 

Please see the Fulcrum Accessibility page for accessibility information about the website and ebook platform at fulcrum.org/newprairiepress. 

The next scheduled accessibility evaluation for this website is in 2027.
