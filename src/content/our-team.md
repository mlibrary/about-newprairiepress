New Prairie Press is an open access publishing imprint of Kansas State University Libraries. Its staff are members of the Libraries’ Open Publishing Exchange (OPE) office.

* [Learn more about the New Prairie Press and OPE team](https://lib.k-state.edu/services-support/scholarly-communication/about/)
* [Contact Us](/contact)

## David Molik, Department Head
David works to advance strategies that enhance the long-term accessibility, integrity and impact of research and scholarly outputs while fostering interdisciplinary collaborations.

## Ryan Otto, Scholarly Communication Librarian
Ryan is the journal and conference coordinator for New Prairie Press. He also coordinates the university's digital repository, [K-State Research Exchange](https://krex.k-state.edu/home), and can help answer questions about digital publishing and [open access](https://lib.k-state.edu/services-support/scholarly-communication/open-access/).

## Carolyn Jackson, Scholarly Education and Open Educational Resources Librarian
Carolyn is the monograph and textbook coordinator for New Prairie Press. She collaborates with faculty and departments across campus to promote and support the development and use of open educational resources, the [Open/Alternative Textbook Initiative (OATI) grant](https://lib.k-state.edu/services-support/scholarly-communication/open-access/textbook-initiative/), e-book publishing and scholarly identity
