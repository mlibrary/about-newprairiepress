## Services Offered
Our journal publishing services are ideal for Kansas State University-affiliated editors seeking a solution to manage article publishing from initial submission through production. Our publishing services are available to active and ceased scholarly and academic publications.  Note that only one editor is required to be affiliated with K-State at the time the journal is onboarded with the Press. The journal itself does not need to be affiliated. 

New Prairie Press offers the following journal publishing services:
* A designed site.  
* Software to manage editorial and peer-review workflows, if needed.  
* DOI registration.
* Training and technical support.
* Upload and hosting of journal back content.
* Information and advice on copyright and licensing.
