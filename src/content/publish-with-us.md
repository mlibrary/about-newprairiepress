New Prairie Press provides publishing services for active and ceased scholarly and academic publications.

Publications from all disciplines - written, edited or sponsored by scholars affiliated with Kansas State University - are welcome at New Prairie Press. In addition to scholarly journals, the Press publishes conference proceedings, faculty and staff authored scholarly monographs, open access textbooks, and other special publications.
